Where to Find Original Documents

The following archives hold original documents, letters, and manuscripts related to the historical figure. Contact the institutions below for more information or to arrange a visit.

National Library Archive

Location: 123 Library St, Capital City

Description: The National Library Archive contains numerous letters and original manuscripts from the historical figure. It is open to researchers and the general public.

Contact: info@nationallibrary.com

University Archive

Location: 456 University Ave, Academic Town

Description: The University Archive houses personal diaries and early drafts of many important works. Access is granted by appointment.

Contact: archive@university.com

Historical Society Records

Location: 789 History Rd, Heritage City

Description: The Historical Society has a collection of speeches, photographs, and other significant materials that document the historical figure's public life.

Contact: records@historicalsociety.com
